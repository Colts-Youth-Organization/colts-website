Earn a spot.

Our auditions typically begin in November, with camps located around the country. We also offer video auditions for those who can't feasibly make it to an in-person camp.

Learn how to join

Travel the country.

The Colts participate in the DCI Tour, performing about about 30 times each summer. Shows are hosted in stadiums all across the country, culminating with DCI Championships at Lucas Oil Stadium in Indianpolis, Indiana.

See the tour schedule

Learn from the best.

Our staff members bring experience from across the activity. From college educators to seasoned drum corps instructors, our educators are prepared to help you improve all season long.

Alumni Information

Thank you for being a part of the Colts legacy! Our goal, ultimately, is to have an as complete as possible list of anyone who has worn the Colts or Colt Cadets uniform from 1963 to now. We are all part of an historic bloodline, and it's important that we are able to keep that bloodline going into the future.

This information is primarily for our internal database. You may receive a few emails or paper mailings from the Colts throughout the year, but the primary use for this database will be for internal record keeping. If you would like your information added to the online database, you can indicate that below. We will post your name, section, years marched, and email online with your permission. You can view the online database here.

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, you can send them to the Colts Alumni Association at alumni@colts.org. Thank you for being a part of the Colts legacy!

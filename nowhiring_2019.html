Hundreds Of Young Lives Each Summer?

The Colts Youth Organization has an immediate opening for a full-time staff position. More likely than not, this position will require residence in Dubuque or the tri-state area. There are many roles left open by the departure of Jason Schubert, Director of Operations & Media, and the desired candidate may fill many of them, but we also may reassign duties within the office depending on the skill sets of the applicant in congruence with the other office employees.

Most notably, the position will require solid writing skills (with a bit of creativity included for good measure). Applicant will be responsible for content development for the Colts weekly email newsletter, as well as regular updates on our social media platforms. We can provide training on applications — such as Salesforce, Pardot, Adobe Creative Suite including Photoshop and Dreamweaver, Google Drive and G Suite applications — although background in any of these areas is definitely a plus. Attention to detail, creative writing, and refined grammatical skills are required at the time of hire. (Sometimes you either got it, or you don’t).

Being resourceful, with the ability to solve one’s own problems, is critical to your success in our small office environment where each spoke on the wheel is interdependent to keep the train rolling smoothly. The ability to successfully find information via Google search is a must. While everything you read on the Internet may not be true, a lot of the information you’ll need is there and available if you know what keywords to type in.

The Colts provide salary compensation and benefits comparable with many area nonprofits. Full-time benefits include 100% Blue Cross Blue Shield health insurance coverage, 403(b) matching contributions, short-term disability insurance, and a monthly cell phone stipend.

Full summer travel is not required as this role may not involve the duties of summer Tour Director, depending on the applicant. The position will be predominantly based out of our offices at 2300 Twin Valley Drive in Dubuque. We are, however, a youth organization who hosts four major summer events (of which only one is in Dubuque) and some summer travel may be necessary.

Alumni are strongly encouraged to apply.

INTERESTED APPLICANTS SHOULD:

1) Email a resume and cover letter to jeff@colts.org, or mail to: Colts Youth Organization, Attn: Jeff MacFarlane, 2300 Twin Valley Dr., Dubuque, IA 52003

2) Send one writing sample of 2-3 paragraphs. This can be something already published, or go ahead and write a brief sample article. This might be a recap of the Colts season, an announcement of an upcoming audition or staff hire, or a flashback to your favorite Colts performance(s) of all time.

3) Include in your cover letter any personal skills that address the qualifications listed in this article, or any other attributes that would make you a great fit to work for the Colts.

Thank you!

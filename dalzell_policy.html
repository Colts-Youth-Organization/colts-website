2018 MUSIC ON THE MARCH REFUND POLICY REVISITED

Because of the total cancellation of the show - in essence, no drum corps were able to perform during the evening - the Colts will honor your ticket stub with a $5.00 coupon for the same event in 2019. Keep your stub and turn it into the office when you purchase next year's ticket(s). Keep an eye on this page as we further develop how the process will work for phone and online sales. Stay tuned!

OR IF YOU PREFER, exchange your Music On The March ticket stub for a FREE ticket to the Tournament Of Drums in Cedar Rapids on Friday, August, 3 2018.

WHY NOT A FULL REFUND?

The Colts host four drum corps shows each summer with events in Dubuque, Des Moines metro, Kansas City metro, and Cedar Rapids. We print on the ticket that events are rain or shine and no refunds will be issued. The reason for this policy is because even in the event of rain, each of the competing units get paid for traveling to our event whether or not they perform. The Colts are obligated to pay our contract to Drum Corps International even in a situation of total cancellation. To avoid a cripling impact on our annual budget with a full refund, we instead provide this $5.00 coupon policy.

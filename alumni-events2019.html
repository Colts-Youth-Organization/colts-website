Friday, June 21 Dubuque, IA Summer Premiere
Alumni are encouraged to attend the ninth annual Colts Summer Premiere on Friday, June 21 at Chalmers Field, University of Dubuque!
The premiere event will be the first full uniform performance from both the Colts and Colt Cadets.
Wednesday, July 10 Ankeny, IA Celebration In Brass
Attend the Colts-sponsored Celebration In Brass returns to Ankeny Stadium on the campus of Northview Middle School!
Alumni should stop by the souvie booth to check in and meet up with other alumni from throughout the years.
Want to help set up an event in Ankeny? Contact alumni@colts.org today!
Friday, July 12 Dubuque, IA Music On The March
Starting at 4:30 p.m., the alumni steering committee will host a tailgate in the parking lot of St. Anthony's (just a few blocks from Dubuque Senior!).
The Colts are also looking for alumni to play the National Anthem before the start of the show. Contact Greg Blum to sign up!
Monday, July 15 Olathe, KS Brass Impact
Attend the Colts-sponsored Brass Impact at the College Boulevard Activity Center in Olathe!
Alumni should stop by the souvie booth to check in and meet up with other alumni from throughout the years.
Want to help set up an event in Olathe? Contact alumni@colts.org today!
Wednesday, August 7 Muncie, IN Annual Banquet
The annual year end banquet is a great opportunity to recognize the achievements made by the Colts and Colt Cadets
throughout the season. A sign up form will be available over the summer to reserve your seat!

Who Are We?

In October of 2009, Pandemonium was formed as a part of the Colts Youth Organization. It is an off-shoot of the pan program offered at Prescott School but not affiliated with any one school.

Any area student in grades 6-12 is eligible to participate and previous experience is not necessary but having taken any music lessons is helpful.

Pandemonium has performed in and around the tri-state area having played at a variety of events and venues. In 2010, Pandemonium was chosen to perform and present a master class to teachers from all across the country at the National Expeditionary Learning Conference in Kansas City, MO.

What is a Steel Drum?

The Steel Drum, or Pan, is a unique instrument, and one of most recently invented. It is a skillfully hammered 55 gallon oil drum which has been carefully tuned to produce tones.

The Steel Drum carries the full chromatic range of notes and can produce just about any type of music you can think of! These instruments are often associated with Calypso type music.

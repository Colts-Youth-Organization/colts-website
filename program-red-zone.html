WELCOME TO THE

RED ZONE

RED ZONE

VIRTUAL INSTRUCTION • EVALUATION • AUDITION

World-Class
Education

The RED ZONE provides all the instruction, education and feedback you need to earn a position in a World Class drum corps. Or, if you need a bit more training or experience, we have an Open Class series to help you develop fundamental skills to set you up for success!

Virtual Instruction

Each online exercise is accompanied by a video to help guide you through the specifics of each lesson. Play along with our educational team and a metronome. Lessons are available for students of all abilities, from beginners to the most advanced performers.

Evaluation

Enrollment in the RED ZONE includes a personal video evaluation by one of our experienced educators. From there, we will work with you to decide what path is right for you. Our team will help you focus on specific improvement areas personalized to your own needs.

Audition & Private Lessons

Participation in the RED ZONE opens up the opportunity to audition for the Colts or participate in Colt Cadets.

The initial audition is a one-on-one lesson with a Colts staff member. Students can enroll in as many additional lessons as desired.

Just curious on seeking guidance or a personal mentor? We will help you find the right educator to guide your growth.

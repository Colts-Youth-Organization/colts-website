Giving Tuesday



As we enter another competitive season, plans are underway to bring a new "face" to a couple of the most important vehicles in the Colts convoy — the equipment trailer and the kitchen trailer. How can you play a part in this once-in-a-generation campaign?

STARTING AT 7:00 A.M. CST, all donations made through Facebook's charitable giving tools will be matched by PayPal, but only until the $7 million in matching funds run out. The gift matching is on a first come, first served basis, so it's important to get your donation in early (7:01 a.m. CST) on Tuesday, November 27. By donating by using Facebook's giving tools, you can double your donation and double your impact on the Colts. Just click on the "X" to help carry the load!

What are the donations for? Thanks to a small group of very generous board members and donors, the Colts have purchased a brand new, custom-built Kentucky brand equipment trailer. The trailer is scheduled to arrive in Dubuque this spring, and we'll have just a few months to build out the interior before it's time to hit the road for the 2019 season. BUT THAT'S ONLY HALF THE STORY! Thanks to an additional generous contribution to the Colts, we already have the funding to purchase a refurbished 53-ft. trailer that will become the new cook truck. We are currently working to raise money for the interiors of both trailers through our 20\20 Vision Capital Campaign.

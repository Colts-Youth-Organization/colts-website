Thank you for your RSVP! A few reminders for you about Colt Cadets weekend rehearsals...

  • If this is your first rehearsal with Colt Cadets, make sure to fill out a Member Application and print and complete the Parent Consent Form prior to rehearsal. This helps save time at check in!
  • The cost for overnight rehearsals is $40. This helps cover the cost of food, housing, and instruction for the weekend. You can pay when you arrive or you can pay early on the Colts Mall. This fee does not apply to summer fees.

Always check colts.org within a few hours of leaving for camp for any last-minute updates! If you need help finding a ride to camp, email Colt Cadets Director Rich Waddington at rich@colts.org.

Have you signed up for our email distribution list? Please do so now. This list is self-generating and you may remove yourself at any time. We also encourage you to have your parents sign up for the list. We use this email list for all correspondence to our members and their families regarding important rehearsal and tour details.

WELCOME TO THE RED ZONE

The Red Zone is our location for students of any age to gain education, receive an evaluation, and participate in an audition.

Our Virtual Learning Center offers instruction and exercises for students interested in improving their proficiency./p>

Enrollment in the RED ZONE is $110 and includes:

  • A Colts Section Exercise Packet. The 2021 packet has printable information, exercises, and details about the Colts technique and approach.
  • Multimedia practice tools. Play along with our instructors, music, and a metronome in a virtual learning system from the convenience of your own home and at your own pace. All materials and methods from our section packets are covered, with additional supplemental practice materials to refine your skills.
  • Video Evaluation. When prepared, submit a video to gain personal feedback from our professional educators. You will be paired up with an instructor most appropriate for your section and skills. The video evaluation is the first step to gain an audition invitation.

(A packet only option is available for $25. This PDF packet contains the exercises included in the RedZone, but without the digital learning tools and evaluation consideration.)

Additional opportunities

Following an invitation to audition for the Colts, a 30-minute live virtual audition lesson will be available for $50. Additional private Colts World Class lessons will be available at $40 per 30-minute lesson. Colt Cadets Open Class lessons will be available as desired at $25 per 30-minute lesson. Students interested in auditioning for the Colts should first join the Red Zone program, prepare the materials, and submit a video evaluation. Feedback from our professional staff will assist you in preparation a live virtual audition lesson. New students in 2021 should plan for at least a second lesson to demonstrate growth in the recommended improvement areas.

ENROLL TODAY

In a time of uncertainty, plan your action in the arts with the Colts Red Zone. Receive inside access to the exercises and techniques used by the Colts. Connect with our World Class staff for feedback and evaluation. Red Zone materials are available for your use through September 30, 2021. Meet and interact with other members throughout the winter months. The Colts Red Zone is your first step to becoming a member of the Colts, Colt Cadets, or preparing for a career in drum corps, marching band, or concert band. The Red Zone is built to help you practice and prepare at your own time, from your own home, in your own way, and without extra pressure of regular assignments.

What you'll find:

  • Music and Materials
  • Video Play Along options (with metronome controls and looping capability)
  • Evaluation and feedback for growth
  • Interaction — Opportunities to engage with new friends

Learn, refine and perfect your skills!

Red Zone FAQs

Who is Red Zone for?

How do I audition for the Colts?

What positions are open in the 2021 Colts?

All positions are technically open. Even with 2020 Colts members returning, we have positions available in all sections.

Can I attend a live event?

With uncertainty surrounding the timeline for in-person audition camps, our current plan is for only virtual activities through December 31. All initial auditions should be completed by December 31.

Do I need to want to be a member of the Colts or Colt Cadets?

Any person of any age, with or without drum corps plans, can engage and improve through our Red Zone activities. Any age is welcome to participate in the Red Zone. Students planning to audition for Colts should be ages 15-21. Colt Cadets are generally 12-17 years of age.

What if I'm a beginner?

Email Colt Cadets Director Rich Waddington rich@colts.org. We have special lessons and tools built for beginning and intermediate students.

What if I'm only interested in Colt Cadets?

Email Colt Cadets Director Rich Waddington rich@colts.org.

What if I have technical challenges?

Email Colts Director of Media and Events Aidan Connolly aidan@colts.org.

What if I'm financially challenged?

Email Colts Director Vicki MacFarlane vicki@colts.org

What if the world gets worse in 2021?

Our focus is on individual growth, since personal motivation, perseverance, and continued development are critical skills required of Colts members. The Red Zone has been built to be beneficial for any individual committed to personal growth.

What if I'm interested in more than one packet?

Email Colts Director Vicki MacFarlane vicki@colts.org. There is no charge to be added to additional and supplemental classrooms.

Is there a deadline to sign up?

No, but it is recommended to sign up early for maximum value. Although Red Zone materials will be available all year long, membership positions will be awarded based on evaluations and auditions beginning in November.

Is my purchase refundable?

No, all sales are final. Please contact office@colts.org if you experience difficulty at checkout.

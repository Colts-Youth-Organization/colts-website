Colts Announce Percussion Instructional Team For 2020

October 15, 2019

The Colts are excited to announce their percussion instructional team for 2020. The team will be led by Percussion Caption Head Josh Nelson and Front Ensemble Coordinator Meagan Nelson. The team brings with them experience from a wide range of backgrounds across all facets of the marching arts.

“I’m really excited about the team we’ve put together," said Josh. "We're looking forward to the audition camps and can’t wait to meet all the future 2020 Colts!”

Joining the Colts this year as battery technicians will be snare technicians Matt Howell and Ben Heidrich, quad technicians Pierson Gilreath and Austen White, and bass technicians Daniel Allen and Taylor Ransom. Returning to the Colts for her second year as cymbal technician will be Dani Cunning. Joining the Colts this year as front ensemble technicians will be Konstantine Vlasis, John Gaal, and Kyle Hammack.

Click here to view the full bios for the 2020 Colts percussion team.

"I am ecstatic for this team and what they bring to the Colts!" said Director Vicki MacFarlane. "Their connectivity will no doubt enhance the experience of our membership, both in the percussion section and the entire corps. The overall education we are able to offer this season is incredible."

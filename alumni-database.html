The Colts Alumni Database includes all alumni of the Colts and Colt Cadets. [Or at least as many as we could find in 50+ years of Music On The March programs*.] If someone has submitted the Alumni Info Form, you'll see a link to their email address by scrolling to the right on the spreadsheet below. You can update your information in our database here! Encourage those you marched with to update their information so we can reconnect the years, and make connections between the decades.

We have hidden email addresses from plain view, and your email address does not appear in the website source code so it will not be picked up by email harvesting bots. We'll do all we can to protect your email privacy while still providing access for alumni to reach out to one another.

If you have any questions, you can contact the Colts Alumni Association at alumni@colts.org.

* We are missing drum corps show programs (Youth On Parade, Round Up Days, Music On The March) for the following years: 1964, 1965, 1966, 1967, 1968, 1971, 1989 and 1990. Any donations to our historical archive would be appreciated.

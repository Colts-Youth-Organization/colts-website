THANKS FOR SIGNING UP!

Thank you for signing up for the Colts Red Zone! To complete your enrollment, please visit the Colts Mall to submit your payment.

Upon purchase, you will be emailed a receipt which includes a link to download our packet and instructions to enroll in Soundslice are on the last page. The link to download the packet will be valid for 7 days after your purchase.

One of our directors will be in contact with you by email soon to welcome you and answer any questions you may have. You can also reach out to us by emailing office@colts.org.

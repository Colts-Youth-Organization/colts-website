Thank you for signing up for Colts Homeroom! One of our directors will be in contact with you by email soon.

You can submit your payment for Colts Homeroom on the Colts Mall.

You will need a Google account to join the Colts Homeroom. This will need to be separate from any Google account provided to you by your school. You will also be invited to join our Colts Homeroom Facebook page, where you can learn from other participants, ask general questions, and "hang out" with like-minded souls in the absence of a summer tour.

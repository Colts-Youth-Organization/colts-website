The tour.

We need volunteers to make this work! Colts volunteers are the lifeblood of the summer tour. Check your schedule and make plans to get out on tour. We are still looking for help on nearly all days of the summer. Please contact Volunteer Coordinator Bill Symoniak, Volunteer Coordinator, by emailing volunteers@colts.org to get on the schedule. Even a day or two helps! Get more details about how to get involved on our Volunteer Page. You can click here to view the Colts Volunteer Calendar for this season.

Check the Colts website often for updates, announcements and, of course, the famous Virtual Cook Truck!

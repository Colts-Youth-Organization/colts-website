2017 Preseason Day & Weekend Rehearsal Dates

Day rehearsals generally begin on Sundays at 1:00 p.m., with unloading and check in at 12:30 p.m., and conclude by 5:00 p.m. Weekend rehearsal camps are scheduled once a month in March, April and May. Rehearsal weekends generally begin at 8:00 p.m. Friday, with unloading and check in at 7:00 p.m. and conclude by 3:00 p.m. on Sunday. Information about weekend rehearsals, including location details, will be posted on the website prior to the weekend

Sunday afternoon rehearsals are free to attend for all students. Weekend camps are $25 per student to help cover the cost of meals and housing.

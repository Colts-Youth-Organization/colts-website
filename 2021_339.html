Colts Present Their 2021 Production — LEAP OF FAITH

April 2, 2021

The Colts are excited to introduce their 2021 production aptly titled, "LEAP OF FAITH." The program includes music by Stevie Wonder & Ariana Grande, ABBA, Paul Williams & Kenneth Ascher, and George Michael.

The Colts are planning 10 performances this summer including the season-ending DCI Celebration in Indianapolis on August 12–14, 2021.

If you’re 16–22 (or 23 after June 1) and interested in marching with us this summer, take a leap of faith and audition today. You can send an Information Request, or go all in and enroll in the Red Zone — our offseason audition program! Learn more about how to get started in the Red Zone HERE. It’s not too late to jump in! We still have openings in many sections.

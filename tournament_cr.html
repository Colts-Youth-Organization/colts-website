Tournament Of Drums celebrates its fifth year as a Colts-sponsored event in partnership with the Iowa Music & Arts Association. In 2019, Tournament Of Drums is one of the final events leading into the DCI Open Class championships. The results of the show will determine the corps order of performance at the DCI Open Class Prelims in Marion, Indiana.

Tournament Of Drums was voted Best Drum Corps International Open Class Tour Event in 2016, 2017, and 2018!

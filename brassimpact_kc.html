Presented by Meyer Music

Brass Impact returns to the beautiful College Boulevard Activity Center in partnership with the Olathe Northwest Band Boosters. All sections to Brass Impact are reserved seating (except General Admission). Tickets are assigned according to the date/time orders are received. Order early to get the best seats!

ACCESSIBLE: Wheelchair accessible seating is available on the bottom row of each section. Small benches are adjoining for guests of wheelchair patrons.

Tickets are ON SALE NOW and begin mailing on May 15. Tickets ordered after Friday, July 3, will be held at Will Call at the stadium box office. Advance ticket sales close on Thursday, July 9, so the ticket operation can be transferred to the show site. Group ticket sales are available through Friday, July 3.

For more information or to inquire about Group Sales, contact the Colts Ticket Desk via email at tickets@colts.org.

Due to the strict travel schedule of the corps, no rain date or refund is available. However, the corps respect the importance of giving their fans a show and will do everything possible to reach that goal.

ORDER NOW!

Groups of 20+ take 10% off. Groups of 50+ take 20% off.

VIP | $38

VIP tickets include more spacious seating atop the center section (orange), a VIP parking pass, and a $5 coupon for concessions or Colts souvenirs.

Super Premium | $28

Super Premium tickets are great seats in the lower center section and top (green) surrounding the VIP section.

Premium | $23

Premium tickets are very good seats in the lower sections just off center and top outer sections (blue).

Reserved | $18

Reserved tickets are value-priced seats in the lower sections (yellow).

General Admission | $18

General admission seating in the outer sections (purple) is available first-come, first-served on the day of the show.

A processing fee of $3.75 is applied to each order.

As winter turns to spring and the excitement of summer draws nearer, the Colts are excited to announce an exciting step forward into a new era of Colts history. World-renowned uniform designer Michael Cesario created a BOLD NEW LOOK for the 2017 Colts.

Upon learning of the possibility of new Colts uniforms in 2017, an anonymous donor approached Board President Peter Hansen with a commitment to provide 1/3 of the cost of the uniforms

In turn, Peter challenged the Colts Board of Directors to personally match the initial contribution. The Board rose to the occasion with a commitment that exceeded the original anonymous pledge.

NOW WE NEED YOUR HELP! Each $85 gift provides the final 1/3 of the cost to outfit one member of the drum corps. Please join us in this effort to bring a NEW VISION to the Colts, and move the corps forward in style! When you pledge $85 or more to the 2017 Colts Uniform Campaign we will e-mail you a complete, full=color rendering of Michael Cesario's sketch via email in thanks for your gift!

The Colt Cadets began their 50th season with two kick-off rehearsals on February 12 and 26. Since then, the group has met almost every Sunday to rehearse and practice for their upcoming season. If you are interested in joining the Colt Cadets for 2017, there's still time! The next rehearsal for the full corps will be Sunday, May 7 from 1:00 - 5:00 pm at Jefferson Middle School in Dubuque. The battery will have an overnight the weekend of April 28 - 30 at Hempstead High School in Dubuque.

Colt Cadets rehearsals are FREE to attend, and there is absolutely no obligation to join. There is no formal audition to be a member, but students are expected to be strong team players with a desire to be great!

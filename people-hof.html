Our finest.

The Colts Hall of Fame was established to recognize and honor alumni members, design and instructional staff, administrators, board members, contributors, or volunteers whose history of outstanding contributions to the Colts has played a significant and long-term role in the Colts and in the marching arts, and whose dedication is worthy of the highest level of recognition by the organization.

The Colts Hall of Fame committee accepts nominations each year beginning April 15th and closing on May 15th. Announcement of the current class of inductees will be made by June 15, with induction ceremonies held at an official Colts event selected by the Colts Executive Director.

Want to nominate someone? Click here to view the Hall of Fame Nomination Process.

Thank you Inductees for the time, love and contributions you have made to the Colts! Your commitment to the marching arts is appreciated beyond words by all who have been Colts, who are Colts, and who will be Colts in the future. We will never forget the legacy you have established and the path you have left for others to follow. You are now permanent members of a very special family whose unique bonds cannot be broken by age or time.

Colts Announce New Partnership With Pageantry Innovations

March XX, 2019

The Colts Youth Organization and Pageantry Innovations are excited to announce a partnership for 2019 and beyond. The Colts will exclusively use Pagaentry Innovations' synth carts, mixer carts, and other products to create their unique productions every season. Colt Cadets will begin transitioning to Pageantry Innovations gear in 2020. Learn more about Pageantry Innovations online at pagaentryinnovations.com

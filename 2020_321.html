Brass Alumni Invited To Join Virtual Hornline

April 28, 2020

The Colts hornline is working to create the next installment of this summer's virtual performances, and we'd like to invite our alumni to join! All you need to participate is your instrument and a camera (the one in your phone works!). If you're interested, send an email to office@colts.org to sign up and receive more information. Submissions are due May 8.

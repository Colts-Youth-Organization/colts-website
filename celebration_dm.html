Celebration In Brass returns to the historic, yet newly-remodeled, Ankeny Stadium, in partnership with the Ankeny Instrumental Music Foundation. All sections to Celebration In Brass (except Accessible) are reserved seating. Tickets are assigned according to the date/time orders are received. Order early to get the best seats!

Tickets are ON SALE NOW and begin mailing on May 15. Tickets ordered after Monday, June 29, will be held at Will Call at the stadium box office. Advance ticket sales close on Monday, July 6, so the ticket operation can be transferred to the show site. Group ticket sales are available through Monday, June 29.

For more information or to inquire about Group Sales, contact the Colts Ticket Desk via email at tickets@colts.org.

Due to the strict travel schedule of the corps, no rain date or refund is available. However, the corps respect the importance of giving their fans a show and will do everything possible to reach that goal.

Five Flags Center

Five Flags Center
Dubuque, IA

405 Main Street, Dubuque (Click for Google map)

The Five Flags Center occupies the entire city block from 3rd to 4th Streets and from Main to Locust Streets. Watch for specific camp information regarding parking and which entrance to use.

From Hwy 61/151 South and Hwy 52 South
Take exit for Locust Street. Continue north on Locust until you reach 4th Street. Five Flags Center will be on your right.

From Hwy 20 East
After crossing the Julien Dubuque Bridge, turn right and stay in one of the two left lanes. Go straight through the stoplight (do not veer right). You are on Locust Street. Continue on Locust to 4th Street, and you'll find the Five Flags Center on your right.

From Hwy 20 West (Dodge Street)
Go west toward the Julien Dubuque Bridge. Stay in the left lane and turn left onto Locust Street. Continue north on Locust until you reach 4th Street. Five Flags Center will be on your right. Watch for specific camp details regarding parking and which entrance to use.

From Hwy 52 North
Once in the city, Highway 52 is Central Avenue traveling south. Continue on Central to 4th Street. Turn right on 4th Street. and continue two blocks to Main Street and turn left. Watch for specific camp details regarding parking and which entrance to use.

Roosevelt Middle School

Roosevelt Middle School
Dubuque, IA

2001 Radford Road, Dubuque (Click for Google map)

From Hwy 20 East
Turn left onto Highway 32 (NW Arterial). At the second stoplight (Pennsylvania Ave.), turn left. Follow Pennsylvania for 0.5 miles and turn right onto Radford Road. Proceed on Radford for 0.5 miles and turn left into the Roosevelt Middle School parking lot.

From Hwy 20 West
Turn right onto Highway 32 (NW Arterial). At the second stoplight (Pennsylvania Ave.), turn left. Follow Pennsylvania for 0.5 miles and turn right onto Radford Road. Proceed on Radford for 0.5 miles and turn left into the Roosevelt Middle School parking lot.

From Hwy 61/151 and Hwy 52 North
Follow signs to Highway 20 West. Turn right onto Highway 32 (NW Arterial). At the second stoplight (Pennsylvania Ave.), turn left. Follow Pennsylvania for 0.5 miles and turn right onto Radford Road. Proceed on Radford for 0.5 miles and turn left into the Roosevelt Middle School parking lot.

Thomas Jefferson Middle School

Thomas Jefferson Middle School
Dubuque, IA

1105 Althauser St, Dubuque (Click for Google map)

IF USING A GPS, do not plug in the address for the school (above), use this address instead: 2525 Viola St, Dubuque, IA 52001.


From Hwy 20 East/West and Hwy 61/151 South
Follow signs to Highway 52 North. This is White Street, one-way going north through downtown. At 20th Street, keep in the right lane and continue to 22nd Street, turn right. Follow 22nd to SECOND stoplight. Turn left onto Windsor Avenue. Three blocks past Sacred Heart Church, take a right on Merz (steep, looks like an alley). Jefferson is at the top of the hill on your left. Before the top of the hill, turn left onto Viola Street and enter the parking lot at the back of the school on your right.

From Hwy 61/151 North
Take exit for 11th Street. Go west (away from river) on 11th St., and then turn right on White St. (Hwy 52 North). At 20th St., keep in the right lane and continue to 22nd St., turn right. Follow 22nd St. to SECOND stoplight. Turn left onto Windsor Ave. Three blocks past Sacred Heart Church, take a right on Merz St. (steep, looks like an alley). Jefferson is at the top of the hill on your left. Before the top of the hill, turn left onto Viola St. and enter the parking lot at the back of the school on your right.
Washington Middle School

Washington Middle School
Dubuque, IA

51 North Grandview Ave, Dubuque (Click for Google map)

From Highway 20 East
Take Grandview Avenue exit off of Highway 20 (Dodge Street). Grandview Avenue is approximately 1 mile from the Mississippi River. After exiting, DO NOT TURN ONTO GRANDVIEW AVENUE. Instead, continue through the stoplight to the next light and turn right onto Lombard St. Enter the parking lot at the back of the school off of Lombard.

From Highway 20 West
Take Grandview Avenue exit off of Highway 20 (Dodge Street). You will see the school from Highway 20 on your left. After exiting, DO NOT CONTINUE TO GRANDVIEW AVENUE. Instead, go to the first stoplight and turn left onto Freemont Ave. Continue through the next light where Freemont becomes Lombard Ave. Enter the parking lot at the back of the school off of Lombard.

From Highway 61/151 and 52 North
Follow signs to Highway 20 West (Dodge Street). Travel west on Highway 20 (away from the river) and take Grandview Avenue exit at the top of the hill. Grandview Avenue is approximately 1 mile from the Mississippi River. After exiting, DO NOT TURN ONTO GRANDVIEW AVENUE. Instead, continue through the stoplight to the next light and turn right onto Lombard St. Enter the parking lot at the back of the school off of Lombard.

Dubuque Senior High School

Dubuque Senior High School
Dubuque, IA

1800 Clarke Dr., Dubuque (Click for Google map)

From Highway 20 East or West
Take Grandview Ave. exit off of Highway 20 (Dodge Street). Travel on North Grandview Ave. approximately 1.25 miles. Turn right on Clarke Drive. You will see Senior High School on your right. A large parking lot is on the northwest side of the school just before the Clarke Dr. and W. Locust St. intersection.

From Highway 61/151 and 52 North
Follow signs to Highway 20 West (Dodge Street). Travel west on Highway 20 (away from the river) and take Grandview Avenue exit at the top of the hill. Turn right onto North Grandview Ave. and continue approximately 1.25 miles. Turn right on Clarke Drive. You will see Senior High School on your right. A large parking lot is on the northwest side of the school just before the Clarke Dr. and W. Locust St. intersection.

Perry - Lecompton High School

Perry - Lecompton High School
Perry, KS

404 Lecompton Road, Perry (Click for Google map)

From Kansas City
Take Interstate 70 West towards Lawrence. Take exit 204 towards E 1450 Road / North 2nd Street. Turn right on to E 1450 Road. Continue on to US Highway 59. After 8.5 miles, continue straight to remain on US Highway 24. Continue for 3 miles and then turn left on to Lecompton Road. The school will be on your right.

From Overland Park and Shawnee
Take Interstate 435 West or South. Take exit 1B for State Highway 10. Continue on State Highway 10 from 31 miles. Turn right onto Massachusetts Street. Continue on Massachusetts Street until it becomes North 2nd Street. Continue on to US Highway 59. After 8.5 miles, continue straight to remain on US Highway 24. Continue for 3 miles and then turn left on to Lecompton Road. The school will be on your right.

From Lawrence
Take North 2nd Street towards Interstate 70. Continue on to US Highway 59. After 8.5 miles, continue straight to remain on US Highway 24. Continue for 3 miles and then turn left on to Lecompton Road. The school will be on your right.

From Topeka
Take US Highway 75, NW Topeka Boulevard, or State Highway 4 north towards US Highway 24. Turn right on to US Highway 24. Continue on US Highway 24 for about 15 miles until you enter Perry. Lecompton Road is on the far eastern edge of town. Turn right on to Lecompton Road. The school will be on your right.

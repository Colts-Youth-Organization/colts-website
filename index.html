Colts Get Relief With Iowa Arts Council Grant

The Emergency Relief Fund Grant will, in part, allow the organization to compensate a portion of its educational faculty who are not full-time employees covered by the Payroll Protection Program.

"We were fortunate to receive some federal funding, and we’ve had invaluable support from our donors," Colts Executive Director Jeff MacFarlane said, "but we have a long way to go to get back on track before next season.”
